The team of Cross Tech created some slide that I really liked in the keep it buisness but keep it in mind:

Here is a corrected and cleanly formatted version:

* **WHO ARE WE?**
  * Team presentation: who, which competencies, why this project?
* **THE CHALLENGE WE ARE TACKLING**
  * What is the problem tackled? Why is this challenge important?
* **OUR SOLUTION**
  * Presentation of the concept, the technology used (or not), and the added value.
* **HOW DOES IT WORK?**
  * Prototype demonstration, explanation of its operation, and use cases.
* **NEXT STEP?**
  * Project growth prospects, market potential, and requirements to move forward.
